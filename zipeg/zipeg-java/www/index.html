Zipeg is the easiest way to open RAR or ZIP archive, find the right file inside archive and unpack it. Zip thru photo archives, see thumbnails, select and extract what you need.


Problems with multipart archives? password protected archives? Please read FAQs


Next version of experimental beta release is usually available at http://zipeg.googlecode.com


Zipeg development is an team of one.
I've worked hard over the years to keep Zipeg going.
Please consider making a donation to support Zipeg.



Zipeg is an ultimate tool for opening ZIP and RAR files on Mac and Windows.

(think of it as a free little brother of WinZip™, WinRar™, StuffIt™ family)



Zipeg supports:

  • Formats: ZIP, RAR, 7z, ARJ, LHA, TGZ, TAR and many many more.


  • Pictures and Photos - preview them instantly or open in external application.


  • Filenames in your national alphabet.


  • Windows: XP, Vista and Windows 7 Mac OS X: Tiger, Leopard and Snow Leopard.
