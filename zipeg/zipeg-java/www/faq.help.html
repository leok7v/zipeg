If you like Zipeg and want to help:

  • You are already helping by using Zipeg.
  • If you experience crashes please do send crash reports it will help to make Zipeg even better.
  • A lot of features were suggested by actual users of Zipeg. If you require something that is not there - please do not hesitate to ask.
  • Spread the word - tell you colleagues, friends family about it.
  • Rate it or write a review on your favorite download site or in a blog.
  • Donations are appreciated and do help to pay for Zipeg hosting and development.
